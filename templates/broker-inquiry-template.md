# Apartment Inquiry Template

For Akhil & Jayshree to send to Boston apartment leasing offices on the 5 shortlisted buildings. Optimized for first contact: establish credibility fast, request a tour for **Saturday, May 23, 2026** (Memorial Day weekend), and get all the disqualifier-grade info in one round-trip so the tour is worth the flight.

Merged from Akhil's own draft (2026-05-10) + the criteria in the project [`PRD.md`](../PRD.md).

## How to use

1. Replace `[ALL_CAPS_PLACEHOLDERS]` with building-specific details.
2. If a question is already clearly answered on the building's website (e.g. amenities page lists a 24/7 gym), delete it — don't waste their time.
3. Most of these buildings have an online "Contact us" form — paste the body into that. If they list a `leasing@` email, send it there directly.
4. Send from Akhil's email; CC Jayshree.
5. Track responses in [`shortlist.md`](./shortlist.md).

Hi [LEASING_TEAM_OR_NAME],

We're a couple relocating from San Francisco — Akhil is starting as a physician at Beth Israel Deaconess (Longwood Medical Area), and Jayshree is starting a faculty role at 216 Massachusetts Ave. Target move-in is around August 1; Akhil's board exam on August 18 makes the timing firm. Akhil will be in Boston the weekend of May 22–24. **Could we tour on Saturday, May 23?** Friday May 22 or Sunday May 24 also work if Saturday is full.

A few questions ahead of the tour so we can come prepared:

**Lease & cost**
3. Lease term options (12, 18, 24 months) — any discount for longer commitments?
4. Some buildings offer medical / academic personnel discounts — is that something you offer?
5. What's included in rent (heat, hot water, electric, water/sewer, trash, internet)? What's billed separately?
6. How much is due at lease signing (first month, last month, security, any fees)?
7. Are there income restrictions? Note: Jayshree's faculty role doesn't start until September 1, so income documentation for that role will lag move-in by about a month.

11. Parking in the building? Garage or surface, and monthly cost?

**References**
18. Would you be open to connecting us with a current resident who could share their experience?

## Variants

**If using a brokerage instead of a leasing office** — swap question 4 (medical/academic discount) for: *"Is there a broker fee, and if so what's the amount and structure?"* Leasing offices don't charge broker fees; brokers usually do.

**If the building's website already shows pricing and pet policy** — drop questions 5, 11, and 15 and lead with: *"We've reviewed your floorplans and pricing — wanted to confirm a couple of details and request a tour."* Shorter = higher reply rate.

---

## Follow-up cadence

- **No reply in 48 hours** → one-line bump: *"Just making sure this didn't get buried — still very interested in seeing [BUILDING_NAME] on May 23."*
- **Tour confirmed** → reply same-day with confirmed time, attendees, and ask for building access / check-in instructions.
- **No 2BR available for Aug 1** → ask if there's a waitlist or if anything is likely to open in the next 4–6 weeks. Mention they're also flexible on a mid-July move-in.
- **They say touring is by appointment only on weekdays** → ask if a 15-min video walk-through is an option for May 23 since Akhil is flying in specifically.
