Tasting menu or seafood-heavy

7-course tasting menu, or empanadas + ceviche + blue crab + fish/cazuela path.

Ceviche plus a rice

Order coconut/lime ceviche, shrimp empanadas if available, then seafood rice or paella.

City notes

  • Use hotel cars, official taxis, or dispatch/car apps.
  • Do not hail random street taxis.
  • Keep phones/cameras discreet in crowded areas and late at night.
  • Use protected ATMs inside banks, malls, or hotels.

Weather + packing

  • July highs around 88°F, lows around 79°F; humidity is heavy.
  • Breathable clothes, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, sandals.
  • One smart-casual dinner/bar outfit for Alma/Carmen/Alquímico.
  • Save offline maps and reservation screenshots.

Every morning

  • Open the current day card and route before leaving Wi-Fi.
  • Check heat/rain, dinner status, and any transfer timing.
  • Screenshot the next address, confirmation, and route.
